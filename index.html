About Me

I'm a junior full stack web developer with a passion for coding, absorbing new skills, and collaborating with other people. I'm looking to join an experienced and talented engineering team focused on challenging, meaningful, and interesting projects that I can contribute to while also learning and growing as a developer.


I previously worked at an e-commerce startup from the ground floor. I started as a Content Writer and transitioned into a management role, heading the written content department as Lead Editor. I was also responsible for general SEO maintenance including best on-page practices and outreach for off-page SEO.


As a means to facilitate content creation, I taught myself basic HTML and CSS to code and style articles for the company's website. I soon became fascinated with the entire coding process from developing the original idea to the final working product. I learned to embrace the rollercoaster of coding. Working in this environment, I began to understand the painful hours spent searching for the best solutions as well as the euphoria of publishing a finished page I could be proud of. It was clear to me that my career path should involve coding, and enrolling in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp was the first step to achieve my goal.


I bring to any endeavor complete and focused dedication, the ability to work with others as part of a team, and a flexible mindset for achieving the success of any project.
